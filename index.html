Welcome to The Ball Factory

Ball Floating Simulator
A simulator where balls bounce off walls at random speeds. The colours of the balls are randomly generated by a set of pallates. When the mouse hovers over any of the balls they grow, and shrink back to normal after leaving the mouse's area of effect. Random velocities and speeds are calculated for each ball.
Ball Bouncing Simulator
A simulator where balls of varying sizes bounce. at various heights. They once again use a randomized colour pallate . The gravity effect make all the balls bounce up and down rythmically at vaying positions.

Instructions

Left Shift: Wipes out all balls
Space: Resets balls and swaps pallates
Left Click: Adds a ball

Ball Sliding Simulator
This page simulates balls of random sizes and speeds scrolling along the speed at random sarting positions. The colours are once again chosen from random pallates. The balls also wrap around the screen intead of them bouncing back off the screen.
